skrutable

For making Sanskrit "less inscrutible" for anyone interested, and because I like Maharashtrian pronunciation. :)

skrutable is a toolkit and online workbench providing transliteration, scansion, and meter identification for Sanskrit text. Although there are other such tools already out there with advantages of their own, skrutable brings several new things to the table:

  • integrated and user-friendly access to several functions in one place
  • modular and documented Python code for use with various projects

I'm hoping this will support a range of users, from beginning students to advanced scholars, in their understanding and enjoyment of Sanskrit language and literature.

I'm also hoping it will help improve the world's stock of digital Sanskrit data. You can help! Check out the "Scan GRETIL" project to learn more.
