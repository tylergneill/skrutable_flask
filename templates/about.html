skrutable

For making Sanskrit "less inscrutible" for anyone interested, and because I like Maharashtrian pronunciation. :)

skrutable is an online workbench providing transliteration, scansion, and meter identification for Sanskrit text. Although there are other such tools already out there with advantages of their own, skrutable brings several new things to the table:

  • integrated and user-friendly access to numerous functions in one place
  • modular and documented Python code meant to be readable and useable for various projects (like this one)

With this, I hope to support a range of users, from beginning students to advanced scholars, in understanding and enjoying Sanskrit language and literature.

I'm also hoping it will help improve the world's stock of digital Sanskrit data. You can help! Check out the "Scan GRETIL" project to learn more.
